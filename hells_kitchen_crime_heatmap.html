NYPD Incident-Level Data (Jan 1 – Dec 31, 2024)
This dataset includes all valid felony, misdemeanor, and violation crimes reported to the New York City Police Department (NYPD).

• Minimum incidents per location: 10
• Point weighting: size and color reflect the total number of incidents at each location
• Intersection offences: geocoded to the crossing's precise X/Y coordinates
• Non-intersection offences: geocoded to the midpoint of the block face
• Station-house exceptions: incidents reported at precinct houses are plotted at the station's coordinates
